Meet the Team

The People Behind the Innovation

A diverse group of students united by passion for robotics, engineering, and making a difference.

Cool Name Pending FTC Team #30439

A Team Built on Collaboration

Cool Name Pending brings together students with diverse talents and backgrounds, all united by a shared passion for robotics and innovation. Each team member contributes unique skills—from mechanical design and programming to business strategy and community outreach.

Our team structure follows the FIRST Tech Challenge model, with students taking on various roles including:

Engineering & Design

Designing, building, and iterating on robot mechanisms using CAD software and hands-on fabrication techniques.

Programming

Developing autonomous and driver-controlled code using Java and the FTC SDK to bring our robot to life.

Business & Outreach

Managing team finances, fundraising, community engagement, and documenting our journey through the season.

Our Values

As a team, we're committed to the core values of FIRST: discovery, innovation, impact, inclusion, teamwork, and fun. We believe that the skills we develop through robotics—critical thinking, collaboration, perseverance, and creativity—will serve us well beyond competition.

Gracious Professionalism

We embrace FIRST's principle of Gracious Professionalism, competing fiercely while treating everyone with respect, kindness, and empathy. We celebrate the success of others and recognize that we're all working together to advance STEM education and inspire the next generation.
